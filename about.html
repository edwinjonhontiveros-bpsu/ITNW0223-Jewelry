The "Bhem" in the Name

The name "Bhem" has derived from a nickname that originated from one of the co-leaders. His idea spurred from his first love, whom he nicknamed such for commemorative purposes. Although the relationship did not bear fruit in the long run, the nickname still remained as the catalyst to the operations -- most of which are recognizable everywhere in the country.
